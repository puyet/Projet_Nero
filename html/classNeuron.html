void Neuron::update_neuron ( double  Iext,
double  Poisson 
)

Update_neuron : it is the main fonction of the simulation of a neuron:.

  1. if the neuron isn't refractory :. two cases possibles:. 1.1. the neuron reaches the treshold, spikes, reset its membran and turn to refractory true. 1.2. the neuron doesn't reach the treshold, first check:. if its ring buffer empty or not, then calcul of its potential of its membrane . with a Iext =0, dt=0.1, Jbuffer wich comes from the buffer, and the background noise's spikes.
  2. if the neuron is refractory : . stay in this state (ie with a potential of its membrane =0) for a refractory time of 20 steps.
  3. increment the localclock at each update .
Parameters
doubleIext: the external current 0 now.
doublePoisson which represents the spikes from the background noise.
